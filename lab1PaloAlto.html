Palo Alto

Palo Alto is headquarters to a number of high-technology companies, including Hewlett-Packard (HP), Space Systems/Loral, VMware, Tesla, Ford Research and Innovation Center, PARC, IDEO, Skype, Palantir Technologies, Houzz, and Lockheed Martin Advanced Technology Center.

Palo Alto has also served as an incubator and as headquarters to several other prominent high-technology companies such as:

  • Google
  • Facebook
  • Apple
